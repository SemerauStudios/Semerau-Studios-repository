Terms and Conditions

  • 1. Scope of Work

    Semerau Studios agrees to provide the requested services as described in the project description. Any additional services requested by the client will be subject to additional charges.

  • 2. Payment Terms

    This section outlines the financial aspects of the agreement, including the general service fee, percentage of sales from advertising, percentage of sales from music, and percentage of sales from merchandise. It ensures both parties are clear on their financial commitments and expectations. It serves to provide a clear understanding of the financial obligations and revenue-sharing arrangements between Semerau Studios and the client, ensuring transparency and mutual agreement on all payment-related terms. Failure to adhere to these terms may result in project delays, additional fees, or termination of services.

    2.1 General Service Fee

    Service fees are determined on a case-by-case basis, depending on the urgency, project size, type, and duration. Once an estimate of the project is completed, a separate form covering a breakdown of projected fees will be provided and must be signed by the client.

    2.2 Percentage of Sales from Advertising

    For advertising services, Semerau Studios will receive a fixed rate of 12% of all sales generated from advertising campaigns. This percentage is in addition to any service fees.

    2.3 Percentage of Sales from Music

    For music-related services, including production, distribution, and promotion, Semerau Studios will receive a fixed rate of 10% of all sales generated from music tracks, albums, and related merchandise.

    2.4 Percentage of Sales from Merchandise

    For merchandise sales, Semerau Studios will receive a fixed rate of 15% of all sales generated from branded merchandise, including but not limited to clothing, accessories, and promotional items.

  • 3. Revisions

    The client is entitled to one revision of the work. Any additional revisions will incur extra charges at the rate of 5%.

Additionally, the final product must retain the Semerau Studios logo watermark, unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

  • 6. Intellectual Property Rights

    Semerau Studios retains all intellectual property rights to any preliminary work, drafts, or concepts created during the project. The client will only have rights to the final product upon full payment.
